The EBNF Grammar for the language

<program> -->program <statements>end_program
<statements>--><state>{; <state>};
<statement>--><assign>| <loop>| <if>
<assign>--><var>= <expression>
<expr>--><term>{(+|-|*|/|%) <term>}
<term>--><var>| (<factor>)
<var>--><id>| <num>
<loop>-->loop(<var>=<var>:<var>)<state>end_loop
<if>-->if( <logic>) <statements>end_if
<logic>--><var>(== | != | >| <| >= | <=) <var >
<id>--><letter>{<letter>|<digit>}
<num>-->digit {digit}
<letter>-->A|B|C|D..|Z|a|b|..|z|
<digit>-->0|1|2|3..|9|
